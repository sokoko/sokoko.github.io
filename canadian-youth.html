Canadian Youth - Build you Career

Canadian Youth, Let's Plan For the Future! "Transparent Canada" collected useful information from the data publish by many government departements that helps you choose your career path. This data includes:

1- Ontario University/College Statistics: This contains the the distribution of majors/specializations across all Ontario Universities. And much more! Check out the data now .

2- Cost of Living: "Transparent Canada" collected the average rent prices of all Canadian cities with population exceeding 10000. This can help you estimate how much you will be paying for housing while studying. Check it out now.

3- Projection of Canadian Employment: Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) uses the models of the Canadian Occupational Projection System (COPS) and the National Occupational Classification (NOC, 2006 version) to develop projections of future trends in the numbers of job openings and job seekers by occupation at the national level. The projections allow for identifying those occupations that may face labour shortage or labour surplus conditions over the medium term. The latest projections cover the 2013 to 2022 period. Transparent Canada aggregated this data to make it easy to easy what is the projected demand on each occupation in Canada. Check it out now
