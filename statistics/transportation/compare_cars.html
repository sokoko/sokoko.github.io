Understanding the Table

Vehicle Model

  • 4WD/4X4 : Four-wheel drive
  • AWD : All-wheel drive
  • FFV : Flexible-fuel vehicle

Transmission

  • A : Automatic
  • AM : Automated manual
  • AS : Automatic with select shift
  • AV : Continuously variable
  • M : Manual
  • 3 – 9 : Number of gears

Fuel Type

  • X : Regular gasoline
  • Z : Premium gasoline
  • D : Diesel
  • E : Ethanol (E85)

Other

Fuel Consumption: Fuel consumption ratings are shown in litres per 100 kilometres (L/100 km).

Fuel (L) / Year: Estimated annual fuel use is based on 20 000 km driven and a combined rating of 55 percent city and 45 percent highway.

CO2 Emissions (g/km): Estimated tailpipe carbon dioxide emissions (in grams per kilometre) are based on fuel type and the combined fuel consumption rating.
